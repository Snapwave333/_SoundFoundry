# SoundFoundry Brand Palette

## Primary Colors

### Background & Surfaces
- **Forge Black**: `#0D0D0F` (RGB: 13, 13, 15) - Primary background
- **Graphite Gray**: `#24262A` (RGB: 36, 38, 42) - Surfaces, panels, borders

### Text
- **Steel White**: `#F3F5F7` (RGB: 243, 245, 247) - Primary text, high contrast

### Accents
- **Forge Amber**: `#FFB24D` (RGB: 255, 178, 77) - Primary accent, CTAs, highlights
- **Resonance Blue**: `#3A77FF` (RGB: 58, 119, 255) - Secondary accent, links, interactive states

### Semantic Colors
- **Success**: `#36C98C` (RGB: 54, 201, 140) - Success states, positive feedback
- **Error**: `#FF4D4D` (RGB: 255, 77, 77) - Errors, warnings, destructive actions

## HSL Values

## Usage Guidelines

### Dark Mode (Primary)
- Background: Forge Black
- Surfaces: Graphite Gray
- Text: Steel White
- Accents: Forge Amber (primary), Resonance Blue (secondary)

### Light Mode (Secondary)
- Background: Steel White
- Surfaces: Light gray variant
- Text: Forge Black
- Accents: Resonance Blue (primary), Forge Amber (secondary)

### Contrast Ratios
- Forge Black on Steel White: 19.5:1 (AAA)
- Graphite Gray on Steel White: 12.8:1 (AAA)
- Forge Amber on Forge Black: 4.2:1 (AA Large)
- Resonance Blue on Forge Black: 4.8:1 (AA Large)

## Social Media Crops

- **1:1 (Square)**: 1080×1080px - Instagram posts, profile images
- **2:1 (Landscape)**: 1920×960px - Twitter header, LinkedIn cover
- **1200×630px**: Open Graph / Facebook share cards
- **1920×480px**: Hero banner (light mode)
- **1024×256px**: Hero banner (compact)

## Logo Usage

### Clearspace
- Minimum clearspace = cap-height of "S" in wordmark
- Never place logo closer than this distance to other elements

### Do's
- Use logo on Forge Black or Graphite Gray backgrounds
- Maintain aspect ratio
- Use approved color variants only

### Don'ts
- Do not skew, rotate, or distort the logo
- Do not add outlines or effects
- Do not use unapproved color combinations
- Do not place on busy backgrounds without sufficient contrast
