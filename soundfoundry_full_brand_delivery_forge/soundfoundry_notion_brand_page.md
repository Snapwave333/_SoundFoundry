# SoundFoundry — Brand System (Neo‑Forge Glow)

**Tagline:** Craft Your Sound.

## Identity
- Logomark: abstract forge waveform with amber→blue gradient strike.
- Wordmark: “Sound” in Steel White, “Foundry” in gradient fill.
- Safe area: clearspace = cap‑height of “S”.

## Colors
- Background • Forge Black `#0D0D0F`
- Surfaces • Graphite Gray `#24262A`
- Text • Steel White `#F3F5F7`
- Accent A • Forge Amber `#FFB24D`
- Accent B • Resonance Blue `#3A77FF`
- Success `#36C98C` • Error `#FF4D4D`

## Type
- Headings: Montserrat SemiBold
- Body: Inter Regular
- Mono: JetBrains Mono

## Voice
Clear, confident, technical; no hype language.

## Usage
- Prefer dark UI. Minimal chrome. Spacing in 8px units.
- Buttons: 6px radius; hover = brightness +4%.
- Do not skew or outline the logo; keep approved colors only.
